About Me

Hi, I'm Aayush Rajesh!

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a passionate photographer based in Mangalore, dedicated to capturing life's most beautiful moments through my lens. With 2 years of experience in the field, my journey with photography began as a hobby and has since blossomed into a lifelong pursuit of creativity and storytelling.



My Vision

Photography, to me, is more than just taking pictures. It's about freezing a moment in time, capturing the essence of a scene, and conveying emotions that words often cannot express. My work is driven by the desire to tell stories—whether it’s the joy of a wedding day, the serene beauty of a landscape, or the candid moments of everyday life.



My Journey

I honed my skills through Nitte Institute Of Communication (NICO). Over the years, I have had the privilege of working with diverse clients, each project adding a unique chapter to my portfolio.
